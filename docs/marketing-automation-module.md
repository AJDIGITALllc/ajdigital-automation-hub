# Marketing Automation Module

## Purpose
Orchestrates and automates marketing campaigns, lead nurturing sequences, and customer engagement workflows across multiple channels for Audio Jones clients.

## Data / Tool Flow
```
Lead Generation → Segmentation → Campaign Automation → Performance Tracking → ROI Analysis
    ↓              ↓               ↓                   ↓                   ↓
Email/SMS      CRM Integration   Multi-channel     Analytics Dashboard  Revenue Attribution
```

## KPIs
- Campaign conversion rates
- Lead-to-customer conversion ratio
- Email open/click-through rates
- Marketing qualified leads (MQLs) generated
- Customer acquisition cost (CAC)
- Return on marketing investment (ROMI)

## Maintenance Cadence
- **Daily**: Monitor campaign performance and automation health
- **Weekly**: Review and optimize underperforming campaigns
- **Monthly**: Comprehensive performance analysis and strategy adjustments
- **Quarterly**: Full system audit and integration updates
